# 🌍 Wind Energy Journey – From Turbine to Household

An interactive learning tool built with Three.js that visualizes how wind energy travels from a wind turbine to a household.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

This project demonstrates the journey of wind energy — from the moment it is generated by a wind turbine, through transmission and distribution systems, and finally into a home.

The goal is to make complex energy systems easier to understand through interactive 3D visualization.

Users can explore how energy flows, how infrastructure works, and how renewable energy becomes usable electricity in everyday life.

---

## ⚙️ Features

- 🌬️ Interactive 3D visualization of wind energy systems  
- ⚡ Simulation of energy flow from turbine to household  
- 🎮 Camera controls using OrbitControls  
- 📊 Debug tools with Dat.GUI and Stats.js  
- ⚡ Built with Vite for fast development  

---

## 🛠️ Technologies

- Three.js
- TypeScript  
- Vite  
- Dat.GUI  
- Stats.js
